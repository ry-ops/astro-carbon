Welcome to **Astro Carbon**, a GitHub Blog-inspired dark theme for Astro.

## Features

This theme includes everything you need to start a professional developer blog:

- **Dark Theme** - Easy on the eyes with excellent contrast
- **Mega Menu** - Category-based navigation with featured content
- **Content Collections** - Organized blog posts with frontmatter validation
- **Responsive Design** - Looks great on all devices
- **SEO Ready** - Meta tags, Open Graph, and structured data

## Getting Started

1. Customize your categories in `src/config/categories.ts`
2. Add your blog posts to `src/content/posts/`
3. Update the layout and footer with your branding
4. Deploy to your favorite hosting platform

## Writing Posts

Create Markdown files with frontmatter:

```markdown
---
title: "Your Post Title"
description: "A brief description"
date: 2025-01-01
category: engineering
author: Your Name
tags: ["tag1", "tag2"]
---

Your content here...
```

Happy blogging!
